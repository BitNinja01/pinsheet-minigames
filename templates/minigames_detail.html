How to play

Goal: Be the first to par all 18 holes on your bingo card.

Marking holes: Click "Log round" to import a round from your history — holes where your net score is par or better are marked automatically. You can also click any hole's "par" or "brd" box to toggle manually.

Net scoring: Your handicap strokes are applied per WHS rules based on each course's slope, rating, and hole index. Higher handicaps receive more strokes on harder holes.

Birdies & payouts: Birdies are tracked separately. When someone wins, each birdie they have earns $1 from every other player on top of the buy-in.

Ties: If the host ends the game with multiple players tied for most pars, the pot is split evenly among winners.
